I'm a Spanish Teacher

I decided to be a teacher due to the lack of educational options and economic limitations in which I lived. It was not my declared vocation. However, with each passing year I found more pleasure in the profession. I spent five years working as a teacher, especially in the rural parts of the region where I lived. That usually happens when you're a novice. The older teachers choose the nearest schools first, and the teachers who come in the rear have to choose to work in the remotest schools.

The Web Design and Development Path

Four years ago I started studying Web Design and Development for two reasons:

  1. Although I started to like working as a teacher, my future was not very promising economically speaking because teachers are poorly paid in Latin America.
  2. I was guided from above to study Computer Science. I can testify that I felt in more than one occasion, how the Lord wanted me to undertake this learning path.

I remember once I was in the Institute library where I started studying computer science, and when I took a PHP book in my hands, I felt a suddenly strong emotion, which for me, was a confirmation from above that this was an inspired decision.
